Indie games is a term that describes games made by independent game makers outside of the big studio developers and publishers. In a sense indie games have existed since the beginning of video game making in one form or another, Rogue has some characteristics of an indie game despite the absense of a large game publishing industry at the time. In the past ten years (2007 - 2017) the indie game world has grown quickly with digital publishing and game making tools allowing independent and hobbyist developers to produce games and distribute them.
